Our Heritage

Shaping Cities for Over 30 Years

Founded in 1989 in Dubai, UAE, ASGC Group has established itself as an industry leader in complex construction deliveries.

ASGC handles large-scale construction assignments for both private developers and governmental authorities. Our vertically integrated system sets us apart: by manufacturing our own steel structure profiles, blocks, precast elements, and ready-mix concrete, we secure complete control over the speed and material parameters of our operations.

Our workforce of over 15,000 engineers and technicians operates with strict safety compliance, ensuring every modern development we build is done responsibly.

Our Vision

To lead the construction industry into a highly efficient, technological future. We aim to be the partner of choice for complex structural developments through innovative vertical supply management.

We work to constantly innovate, adopting Building Information Modeling (BIM), 3D printing parameters, and modern prefabrication techniques to elevate the pace of local construction.

Our Mission

To deliver turnkey landmarks that safely meet our clients' requirements, budgets, and green development targets.

We guarantee quality control and mitigate materials delays by sourcing structural components, custom glazing panels, and specialized mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) layouts from our own family of subsidiaries.

Committed to Sustainable & Green Development

As pioneers in modern construction methods, ASGC prioritizes eco-friendly structures. We specialize in building under LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) and Estidama green building rating systems to ensure minimized carbon footprints and highly efficient resource consumption.

Our construction sites integrate digital waste tracking, solar hybrid power generators, and sustainable raw material sourcing, helping our clients achieve prestigious green architectural credentials.
