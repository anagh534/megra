Who We Are

Empowering Construction with Vertical Integration

As a leading construction conglomerate, ASGC controls its supply chain to guarantee unmatched precision and efficiency.

Established in 1989 and headquartered in Dubai, UAE, ASGC has grown into a vertically integrated construction powerhouse. We offer turnkey services across the entire construction lifecycle, ensuring quality control, timely delivery, and operational agility.

Our control over key supply-chain components—including ready-mix concrete, steel fabrication, facade engineering, and full mechanical, electrical, and plumbing (MEP) services—sets us apart as a highly reliable general contractor.

Vertical Integration

How We Manage the Supply Chain

By controlling a large portion of our supply chain through key in-house subsidiaries, ASGC manages and mitigates typical construction delays, logistics risks, and resource shortfalls.

Our structural steel, ready-mix concrete, block manufacturing, high-performance facade glazing, and MEP specialists work cohesively under a unified corporate team.

This structure yields maximum reliability, higher speed, and robust quality assurances for developers.

Subsidiary Network
01

Design & BIM Engineering

Utilizing high-end Building Information Modeling (BIM) to plan, coordinate, and execute structure parameters before ground-breaking.

02

Materials Manufacturing

Production of structural steel (ASGC Steel), high-grade ready-mix concrete, pre-cast panels, and concrete block masonry in-house.

03

Glazing & Specialized MEP

Custom engineering of high-performance glass facades and specialized MEP installation by fully owned expert subsidiaries.

04

Turnkey Delivery & Management

Project coordination, construction site assembly, fit-out detailing, and project financing solutions rolled into one turnkey delivery.

Committed to Sustainable & Green Development

As pioneers in modern construction methods, ASGC prioritizes eco-friendly structures. We specialize in building under LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) and Estidama green building rating systems to ensure minimized carbon footprints and highly efficient resource consumption.

Our construction sites integrate digital waste tracking, solar hybrid power generators, and sustainable raw material sourcing, helping our clients achieve prestigious green architectural credentials.
